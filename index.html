Welcome to SNAMES

The Society of Naval Architects and Marine Engineers, Singapore (SNAMES) was formed in 1981 to facilitate the exchange of ideas on the practical and scientific aspects of Design, Construction, Operations, Repairs and Maintenance of Marine Machinery, structures, vessels and related fields.

Apart from being an organisation with solid knowledge base, it is also an excellent organisation for members to network in the Marine and Offshore industry.
